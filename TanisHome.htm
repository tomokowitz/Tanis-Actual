What is Tanis?
Tanis is different things to different people.

To me, before listening to the Tanis podcast (1) , it was just an

ancient and possible fictional lost city in Egypt

where much of the film "Raiders of the Lost Ark"

took place.
After listening avidly for several months, however,

I am beginning to suspect that it may be more

than just on-demand, serialized audio entertainment.

I believe there may be a conspiracy within this dramatization

and synthesis of multiple conspiracy theories simultaneously.
The Call of Tanis?
Considerable parallels can be drawn between the subject matter of

writings of H.P. Lovecraft(2) with major plot elements of the Tanis

series. An inner pattern within these references, once revealed,

seems to betray a clandestine but mass-scale

distribution of subliminal messaging aimed at its

audience’s subconscious minds, more specifically the cortex.

The cortex is the part of the brain that generates the content of

dreams.(3)
Lovecraftian Influence on Tanis
Perhaps the most obvious if less significant parallel is the similarity in primary images in both series:
the tentacled visage of Cthulhu and the Old Ones in Lovecraft’s mythos, and the scientific interest
regarding cuttlefish and cephalopods in general during one episode of Tanis.
